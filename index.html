About Karo Pitch

Bridging the Gap Between Bharat's Founders & Investors

Thousands of founders across India are building amazing businesses — from D2C brands to tech startups — but they lack access to investors, mentorship, and visibility. Karo Pitch connects these founders with investors through curated pitch events and a startup discovery platform.

Structured Pitch Platform

A closed-room investor pitch event where selected startups present their businesses directly to investors.

Bharat-First Mission

Empowering founders from Tier-2, Tier-3 cities who are building real, impactful businesses but lack access to the startup ecosystem.

India's Startup Discovery Hub

Building India's most accessible startup discovery and funding platform where every great idea gets a fair chance.

How It Works

From Application to Funding in 4 Simple Steps

Our streamlined process ensures every deserving startup gets a fair chance to pitch, impress investors, and secure the funding they need to grow.

Step 1

Apply with Your Pitch Deck

Submit your startup details and pitch deck through our simple application form.

Step 2

Get Shortlisted by KaroStartup

Our expert team reviews applications and shortlists the most promising startups.

Step 3

Pitch Live to Investors

Present your business in a structured, closed-room pitch session in front of top investors.

Step 4

Raise Funding & Scale

Connect with the right investors, secure funding, and take your startup to the next level.
